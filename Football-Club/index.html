About The Academy

Structured training for consistent growth

Elite Football Academy focuses on providing young players in Vadodara with a clear pathway. We apply structured coaching methods and fitness science to support every stage of a player's development.

Professional Coaching

AIFF-licensed coaches deliver structured, age-appropriate sessions built on real club curricula.

Tactical Development

Game-intelligence training — positioning, decision-making and pattern play — from U12 upward.

Fitness & Performance

Sports-science led conditioning covering speed, strength, recovery and injury prevention.
