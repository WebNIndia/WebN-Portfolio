Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to what most first-time patients ask us.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

Yes, we welcome walk-ins, though booking an appointment in advance helps us minimize your wait time and prepare for your visit.

Is teeth whitening safe for enamel?

Our professional whitening treatments are dentist-supervised and formulated to protect enamel while delivering visible, lasting results.

How long does a dental implant procedure take?

The full process, from placement to final restoration, typically spans 3–6 months depending on healing and individual bone density.

Do you offer emergency dental care?

Yes, we reserve same-day slots for dental emergencies. Call our clinic directly and our front desk will prioritize your visit.

At what age should children start dental visits?

We recommend a first visit by age one, or within six months of the first tooth appearing, to establish healthy habits early.

Do you accept insurance and payment plans?

We work with most major insurance providers and offer flexible in-house payment plans for larger treatments.
